Human Endogenous Retrovirus Analysis (HERVanalysis)

Overview: This project investigated the transcription of Human Endogenous Retroviruses (HERVs) in melanoma cells under serum starvation stress.

Technologies: Python, R (ggplot2, BioMart), Telescope, Bowtie2, Samtools, IGV.

Outcome: Provided novel insights into stress-induced genomic responses.

Overview: Re-analyzed single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) data from metastatic breast cancer to explore cellular heterogeneity.

Technologies: R (Shiny, Seurat), Python, STAR, Samtools.

Outcome: Enhanced understanding of tumor heterogeneity.
